Meet the Team

Samantha Villenueva So

position: Founder / Certified Lash Technician

About me: Samantha Villanueva So began working in the beauty industry since 2017 where she originally founded her company previously named Lashedbysammievee as a certified Lash Artist. While mainly focusing on Eyelash Extensions, Samantha had always loved the beauty industry and everything about it. Samantha at first started doing lashes as a therapeutic way to manage her stress while planning for her dream wedding, As she continued to take on more clients she started to realize that this was something more than just a hobby it turned into a passion. Shortly after she started lashing, her clientele had grew dramatically. 3 years later Samantha has built SoBeautiful &Co Beauty Bar that not only focuses on Eyelash extensions but cater to other beauty services such as Spray Tanning, Cosmetic Teeth Whitening, Brow Lamination & Make up. Samantha prides herself in providing only the very best to her clients and promotes the highest standards of health and safety. Samantha continues to educate herself by attending training workshops, researching and connecting with her clients to ensure her skills are up to date on the current trends and products.

Hailey Braga

position: Head Makeup Artist

About me: Hailey Braga is a Makeup Artist from Mississauga, Ontario. At a young age you could find Hailey playing around with hair and makeup. Hailey’s Passion for makeup grew much more in Highschool where you could always find her practicing the latest make up trends on her herself ,friends & family. With almost two years of freelance make up experience she is excited to share her passion with the world. “I love enhancing other’s beauty and making them feel even more confident about themselves. I can’t wait to glam more beautiful faces and make you feel like the boss babe that you are!”
